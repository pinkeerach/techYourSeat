Our Movement

We are a coalition of the Charlotte women in tech meetups whose goal is to cultivate a centralized movement of increased diversity in the Charlotte community. As part of this movement, we are supporting adult women at varying points in their career.

Whether you are just entering the workforce, embarking on a second or third career, or have been in tech for a while, we are here to support and inspire you. Advocates are a crucial part of this movement, which is why we also welcome and support WIT and STEM advocates.
